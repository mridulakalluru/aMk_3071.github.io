Art
DANCE

At the heart of my artistry lies the dance form of Kuchipudi. With roots tracing back to the sacred pages of Indian tradition, Kuchipudi is storytelling, a living narrative of cultural history. It's a dance style that beautifully merges graceful movements with intricate expressions, evoking tales of gods, goddesses, and the human experience.

For over 17 years, I have immersed myself in the world of Kuchipudi. I've honed my craft with boundless devotion, embarking on countless solo and group performances that have taken me on a journey through the intricate rhythms and stories of this classical dance form.

Under the tutelage of Saikantha Raparala, I have not only learned the delicate nuances of dance but also the profound importance of cultural preservation and storytelling.
